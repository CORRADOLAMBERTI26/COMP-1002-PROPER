Need help?

Try these quick checks before seeking repairs:

  • Make sure the batteries have power and are inserted correctly.
  • Ensure the cassette is fully seated inside the player.
  • Check that the headphones are firmly plugged in.

Frequently Asked Questions

Sound is distorted: Try cleaning the tape head or testing a different cassette.

Device won't turn on: Replace the batteries and double-check the polarity labels.
