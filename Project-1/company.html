Who is the company behind the Walkman?

Sony Corporation is a Japanese electronics company known for innovation in consumer technology. From radios and tape recorders to televisions and studio equipment, Sony has shaped how the world listens and watches.

The Walkman TPS-L2 was a breakthrough in miniaturized audio technology, designed to let people enjoy music anywhere.

Corporate values

  • High-quality sound for everyday life.
  • Functional, beautiful product design.
  • Technology that feels personal and approachable.

The Walkman reflects all of these values, turning music into a personal experience.

Inspiration for this web site

This website imagines how Sony could have introduced the Walkman TPS-L2 if the internet had existed in 1979. Instead of relying only on magazines and brochures, customers could explore support, features, and reviews online.
