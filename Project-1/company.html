Who is the company behind the Walkman?

Sony Corporation is a Japanese electronics company dedicated to creating innovative audio and video products.

The Walkman TPS-L2 builds on Sony’s experience in cassette recording and miniaturization to deliver a new way to experience music.

Corporate values

  • Bringing high-quality sound into everyday life.
  • Designing products that are both functional and beautiful.
  • Making advanced technology approachable for ordinary listeners.
