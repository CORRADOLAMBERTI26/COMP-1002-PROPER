Who is the company behind the Walkman?

Sony Corporation is a Japanese electronics pioneer known for creating innovative consumer technology. From early radios to modern entertainment systems, Sony has shaped how people enjoy music, movies, and media.

The Walkman TPS-L2 was one of Sony’s most influential inventions, introducing the idea of private, portable music for the first time.

Corporate Values

  • Delivering high-quality sound for everyday life.
  • Designing products that are functional and visually appealing.
  • Creating technology that feels personal and easy to use.
